## Tickets

### How much does it cost?

Our tickets will be priced as follows:

- Early bird (until and including 15 June): €60
- Regular (until and including 21 September): €80
- Late (until the day before): €90

Head on over to [the tickets page](../tickets) to get your ticket!

### Are tickets refundable?

Yes, tickets are refundable up to two weeks before the conference date.
[Contact us](mailto:contact@buildingbruges.be) for more info.

### Can I get a VAT invoice?

Sure! [Contact us](mailto:contact@buildingbruges.be) for more info.

## Catering

### What if I have special dietary requirements?

Don't worry! One of our founding members is a vegetarian so there will be
vegan options. If you have any allergies, please contact us and we will
make sure you can enjoy the conference as well.

## Mobility

### Is the conference wheelchair accessible?

Yes! There will be reserved parking spaces and the building itself is
fully wheelchair accessible (including the toilets).

## Sessions

### Will the sessions be recorded?

We will not be recording the sessions.

### Will slides be shared afterwards?

This is up to the speaker.

### What language are the talks give in?

The talks will be given in English.
