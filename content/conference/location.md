The conference is located at "Onderwijscentrum d'Abdij" 
([Baron Ruzettelaan 435, Bruges](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Y58aRBdFnG9vBPnG9)),
a historic abbey.

## Coming By Bicycle

You can park your bicycle in the bicycle racks at the back of the venue. You
can reach these racks through the parking at the right of the church.

There are also some bicycle racks at the front of the venue.

## Coming By Car

There are three options to park your car:

- On the public Park & Ride next to the bridge, which has 185 parking
spaces. It's a 250m walk to the converence venue.
- To the right of the church, right next to the conference venue (45 spaces).
- At the side of the road (Baron Ruzettelaan).

![](/images/conf/mobility.jpeg)

These three options are free of charge.

## Accessible Parking Space

The parking next to the church (closest to the venue) has several
accessible parking spaces reserved for those who need it.
